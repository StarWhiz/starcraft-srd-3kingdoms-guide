I wrote this guide for normal mode because I felt bad for ditching a lobby of 5 for a 5 man hard lobby.

Anyways it's possible to 3 man normal mode, if all players have mythic medic unlocked.

A little bit of luck is required which means having one player pull Hua Tao (Zerg Kerrigan) healer before round 70.

The healer will need to work towards mythic method for the round 90 boss.

# Before RD 10
Just get as many flags as you can. Try to get flash T3 bonus but don't fret if no one gets it.

# RD 10 Boss
3 players with T2 units can clear this boss easily. 

The idea is to not have any DPS downtime by [swapping in place](https://github.com/StarWhiz/starcraft-srd-3kingdoms-guide#tanking-for-your-dps-by-swapping-in-place) to tank.

# Before RD 25
Just focus on getting flags again and have at least 7 upgrades on T3-T4. Try to get a T4 unit before or during RD25 for DPS.

# RD 25 Boss
Usually at least one player will have a T4 unit here. Just have the other T3 players tank by swapping in place.

T4 can occasionally tank.

# Before RD 55
Build at least one T5 unit before the boss fight with at least 7 upgrades. The more upgrades the merrier.

# RD 55 Boss
3 Players with 3 T5 units can clear this on 7+ upgrades. You need to apply the [set up trick](https://github.com/StarWhiz/starcraft-srd-3kingdoms-guide#rd-55-boss-cheesing-guide) and wipe out the Marine first. Then rush for the SCV, Zergling and Broodling bosses.

It helps if you take turns tanking the marine to reduce DPS downtime. Swap in place to your T4s if you must.

#  RD 70 Boss
One of the players needs a Zerg Kerrigan Healer (Hua Tao) before this boss fight. If none of the players have this this is where the round ends and you will have made a little bit more EXP than an easy clear.

You just need one Zerg Kerrigan Healer (Hua Tao) and 2x T6 minimum to clear this boss.

Take turns sharing damage from a distance (not up close). This way all players have time to react to the sound cue and dodge the bullet bullet hell and exploding circles.

# RD 80 Boss
You will need a player with a mythic hero which AOE that can clear the zerg hallucinations here. The other one can be a Tier 7 with decent AoE.

At this point just make sure you gather all the hallucinations in one place, and have the mythic AoE them all with a skill.

The healer sound focus on survival during hallucianation mode while pulling aggro from the hallucinations at the edge of the map and bring them towards the mythic hero. Mix blinking and running and save heal (preferably heal your teammates at the same time) to survive.

# RD 90/90 Before Boss
Have the Zerg Kerrigan Healer (Hua Tao) fuse into the mythic medic mid wave and get ready to fight the boss.

# RD 90 Boss
Non medic players should have Mythics, T7s, T6s, and extra T5/T4 units hotkeyed and ready to quick swap.

The reason for this is because the medic can only escape and blink heal units that have a heart icon. Both non medic players need to constantly swap units so that there is always a full heart icon on their unit.

![](/media/full-heart-vs-cooldown.png)

The medics blink has unlimited range however she can only blink to targets with full hearts. It's important that each non-medic players swaps in a unit with full hearts so that when the boss shoots a missile, the medic can dodge it by blinking to an ally with full heart.

The medic should avoid blinking to team mates if a missile is about to shoot out from the boss. Just wait for the missle to be over then spam blink heal like mad to get hp back.

The medic shares team damage and cannot fully heal her self if the other two players don't constantly swap.

Each time she heals a teammate by 50% she heals by a certain percentage. So maxxing the heart uptime is mandatory for beating the round 90 boss comfortably.
