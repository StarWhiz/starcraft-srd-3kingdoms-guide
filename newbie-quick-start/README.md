# Music Disabling
You can disable the music with the `INSERT` button on your keyboard once the game starts.

## Combination Basics
- It takes 4x same Tier 1 units to make random Tier 2 unit.
- It takes 4x same Tier 2 units to make a random Tier 3 unit.
- It takes 3x same Tier 3 units to make a random Tier 4 unit.
- It takes 3x same Tier 4 units to make a random Tier 5 unit.
- It takes 2x same Tier 5 units to make a random Tier 6 unit.

### Automatic Sort and Combine
- Click on the psi emitter and press QWE one time each it will light up the psi emitter.
- Buttons: Auto Tier 1 (Q) / Auto Tier 2 (W) / and Auto Tier 3 (E)
- Other Buttons: Auto-Sell Tier 1s (A)
- You can't auto combine Tier 4 with this method

![](/media/1-autocombine.png)

### Manual Combine
This can be done for any tier unit. But is required for tier 4 and higher.

Follow along the screenshot to find the manual combine button on your unit. Tier 4 requires 3 of Tier 3s.

Hovering over the button tells you how many units you need left to combine.

### Line Bosses or Individual Boss Waves
This is the most important part of the game. If you don't kill your line bosses in time you will be behind. 

Killing the line bosses give you unit tokens that you can use to buy units of the same tier as the token.

These boss waves appear at RD3, RD5, RD10, RD15, RD20, RD25, RD30, RD35, RD40, RD55, go on cooldown and respawn again after some time.
#### Kite the Boss
You need to kite the boss by following this screenshot below. Move and press S to hold position. 

The units will auto target the line boss when close enough.
![](/media/7-kitingminibosses.png)

### Spending Scarabs (Selection Tokens)
Second most important part of the game. You'll earn tokens as you kill your line bosses.

Click on the scarabs to bring up the scarab shop. You can cycle thru pages to spend different tier scarabs on different tier units.

Use this to help complete missing units and finish unit combinations.

- Grey Token = Buy any T2 unit
- Blue Token = Buy any T3 unit
- Yellow Token = Buy any T4 unit
- Green Token = Buy any T5 unit

![](/media/8-tokenselection.png)

## Tanking for your DPS by Swapping In Place
If someone is hard carrying you with higher tier heroes. You would be most useful tanking the boss damage for your DPS.

You can swap units in place while taking damage. Let's say your first unit is in control group 1. Hot key your other units in control groups 2-6 for example. Once your main unit is low health. Press 2 to select the other unit and press D to swap in place. That way you don't have to move your screen to swap units.

You can cycle thru this. Press 3 and press D to swap in place unit 2. Press 4 and press D to swap in place unit 3. Press 5 and press D to swap in place unit 4. By this time your main unit will be fully healed which can you can swap back in place by pressing 1 and D.

RD 40 - One of each T4 Unit
